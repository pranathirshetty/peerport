How it works

Getting the help you need is simple. PeerPort connects you with a community of peers and seniors in just a few clicks.

Here's how you can get started:

Step 1:
  • Sign Up & Create Your Profile
  • Create your free account and let us know a little about you.
  • Share your major, your courses, and your academic goals.
Step 2:
  • Find Your Community
  • Search for the help you need. You can join:
      Study Groups: Find students in the same courses to study with.
      Collaboration Spaces: Team up with peers on projects and assignments
      Mentorship Programs: Connect with experienced seniors or alumni for one-on-one guidance.
      Resource Sharing: Access a library of shared materials and resources.
Step 3:
  • Start Collaborating
  • Ask a question in an anonymous forum
  • Share resources and insights in a dedicated repository
  • Schedule a group study session with your new team.
Step 4:
  • Succeed Together
  • PeerPort is more than just a tool—it's a community. By giving and receiving help, you're not just succeeding in your studies; you're building a network and developing valuable skills that will last a lifetime.
